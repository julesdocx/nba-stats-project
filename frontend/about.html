At first I had a search bar to search all players, but for now I listed some famous players.


How does it work?

  • you select the data you want on your chart
  • you can reset the chart with the reset chart button
  • you press save to go to the form.html
  • you fill in the form and save the chart

  • It's saves your chartData and the form data in the dataBase
  • your saved charts are displayed underneath MY CHARTS
  • you kan delete a chart by pressing the cross beside it

  • you can load your previous charts by clicking on them
  • you can update the chart by clicking on save chart again

  • extra: the class and mudule are used in form.js
